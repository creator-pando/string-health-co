Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Water Drink Reminder (the "App") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

The App itself does not directly collect personal information. Your water intake data is stored only on your device.

To provide advertising, the App includes the Google AdMob SDK, which may collect the following information:

  • Advertising identifier (Advertising ID)
  • Device information (OS version, device model, etc.)
  • IP address and general location
  • App usage data

2. How Information Is Used

Collected information is used to serve personalized ads and measure ad performance. The App developer does not have direct access to this data.

3. Third-Party Services

This App uses Google AdMob for advertising. You can review Google's privacy policy here:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Retention

Water intake data stored by the App remains only on your device and is deleted when the App is uninstalled. No data is transmitted to any server.

5. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
